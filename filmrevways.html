31 ways to generate revenue in 11 categories

  1. Theatrical release
  2. DVD (purchase and rental).  
  3. Pay for view and pay by broadcast/satellite/cable.  
  4. Downloading from Internet to desk top computers, cell phones and TV, whole and serialized
  5. Specialty markets (Airline showings ; Armed forces base showings ; Schools and college showings.   
  6. Licensed merchandize related to books and coloring books
  7. Licensing of products based on the film:   boats, rods, reels, jewelry, mugs, toys
  8. Licensing of film characters: toys, dolls, action figures, clothing lines.
  9. Licensing to fast foot chains to selected film based products as listed above
  10. Open film in conjunction with a major bass fishing tournament for maximum exposure and publicity
  11. Replicating the above in as many countries as possible, especially those with a great interest in bass fishing, e.g., Japan and Asia.   In other words, due to the world wide interest in and passion of bass fishing, the film will generate box office and DVD interest around the world as it entertains the global economy.

  2. Theatres:   art houses (i.e., Landmark Theatres)
  3. Home video/DVD purchases:  
  4. Home video/DVD rentals (different sums reported but great sums either way)

  5. Internet film / other content down loads:   Homes; wireless areas
  6. Internet film/other content   down load: Non-homes (offices, schools, military bases, ships at sea)
  7. Internet film/other content downloads:   IPODS, cell phones, other hand helds
