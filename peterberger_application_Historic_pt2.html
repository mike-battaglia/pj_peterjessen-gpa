More on “historically specific conservatives” and “historically non-specific conservatives”

FROM:   Solution Paper #44, posted May 21, 2011, Guidelines for Including Justice in Planning Meetings to Calculate a Better Future for Minneapolis in terms of education, jobs, housing and public safety, by engaging in a “ contestation" between competing public policy idea sets.
